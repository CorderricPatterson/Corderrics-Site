About Us

Hello! I'm Corderric Patterson, proud owner and founder of Corderric's Pressure Washing. I started this journey in 2023 with the help and support of my father who provided me with the tools to bring my dream to life.

Since then, I've been serving customers in my hometown with top-quality pressure washing services. Whether it’s driveways, patios, fences, or building exteriors — we make sure your property shines like new!

My mission is to deliver outstanding results with integrity, hard work, and attention to detail. I am committed to growing and expanding into nearby cities, while maintaining the same high-quality service that our clients know and trust.

Thank you for considering us. We hope to be your #1 choice for pressure washing needs!

Corderric Patterson - Owner & Founder
